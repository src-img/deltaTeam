Welcome to deltabeatz!

This tutorial will show you how to use the application.

Trackbar

This trackbar contains a play button, save button, metronome, and sound select. These can be used to interact with a composition.

Record Compositions

To record a composition, click the red recording button. 4 clicks will play and a countdown will occur, then you can start tapping the key "A" to the beat of your rhythm!

Once finished, click the red recording button, and the recording will stop.

Playing the Composition

Once you are done recording, you can click the "Play" button in the top left to hear what you input! You can also change the sound that is played.

You're ready to go!

If you need any more help, click the little gnome buddy down in the bottom for more help. Have fun!
