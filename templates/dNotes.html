A note is a symbol that tells a musician to play a specific sound. Notes show what pitch to play (how high or low a sound is) and how long to play it. Notes are written on a set of five horizontal lines called a staff, and where the note sits on the staff determines its pitch.

Each note has a name, like A, B, C, D, E, F, or G. After G, the pattern repeats! So the musical alphabet is just these seven letters in a loop.

In the case of DeltaBeatz, we are only focusing on the rhythms, not the note pitches, as everything rests on the same line.
